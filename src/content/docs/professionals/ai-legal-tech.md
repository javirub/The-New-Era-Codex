# AI for Legal & Contracts

## Contract Review

```
Prompt: Review this contract:

[Paste contract]

Analyze for:
- Key terms and obligations
- Potential risks
- Unfavorable clauses
- Missing provisions
- Ambiguous language
- Market standard comparison

⚠️ Not legal advice - consult attorney
```

## Document Drafting

```
Prompt: Draft [NDA/Service Agreement]:

Parties: [Company A] and [Company B]
Purpose: [Describe]
Key terms: [List]
Jurisdiction: [State/Country]

Include standard clauses for:
- Definitions
- Obligations
- Term and termination
- Confidentiality
- Liability
- Governing law
```

## Legal Research

```
Prompt: Research legal question:

Question: [Legal issue]
Jurisdiction: [Location]
Context: [Situation]

Provide:
- Relevant laws/regulations
- Case precedents
- Common interpretations
- Risk factors
- Recommended approach

⚠️ Verify all citations
```

## Clause Library

```
Prompt: Generate clause:

Type: [Indemnification/Warranty/etc]
Context: [SaaS agreement/Services/etc]
Favor: [Balanced/Provider/Customer]

Provide:
- Standard clause
- Alternative versions
- Explanation
- Considerations
```

## AI Legal Tools

- **Harvey AI**: Legal AI assistant
- **CoCounsel**: Legal research
- **LawGeex**: Contract review
- **Spellbook**: Drafting assistant
- **Kira Systems**: Due diligence

## Important Disclaimers

⚠️ **Critical**:
- AI is NOT legal advice
- Always consult qualified attorney
- Verify all information
- Jurisdiction-specific
- High-stakes = human review
