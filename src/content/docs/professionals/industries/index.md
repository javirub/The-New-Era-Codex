## Industry Highlights

**Healthcare**:
- HIPAA compliance requirements
- Patient privacy protections
- Clinical decision support limitations
- Documentation standards

**Education**:
- Student data privacy (FERPA)
- Academic integrity considerations
- Accessibility requirements
- Differentiated instruction support

**Real Estate**:
- Fair housing compliance
- Market data accuracy
- Disclosure requirements
- Client relationship management

**Nonprofits**:
- Mission alignment
- Donor stewardship
- Impact measurement
- Resource constraints

**Supply Chain**:
- Multi-party coordination
- Real-time data integration
- Risk mitigation
- Sustainability tracking

<Aside type="tip">
Industry-specific AI applications often require:
- Domain expertise for proper implementation
- Industry-specific training data
- Compliance with sector regulations
- Integration with specialized software
</Aside>
