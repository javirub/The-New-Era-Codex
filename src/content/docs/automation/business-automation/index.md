Supercharge your business operations by automating repetitive tasks with intelligent AI workflows.

## What You Can Automate

- **CRM**: Auto-update contacts, score leads, send follow-ups
- **Calendar**: Smart scheduling, meeting prep, reminder management
- **Meetings**: Transcription, summary generation, action items
- **Analytics**: Automated reporting, insight generation, alerts
- **Notifications**: Intelligent alerts based on patterns and priorities

<Aside type="tip" title="Time Savings">
Business automation can save 5-15 hours per week per employee by eliminating manual data entry and repetitive tasks.
</Aside>
